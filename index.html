Terri's Top Treatments

Welcome to Terri's Top Treatments

I specialize in slipcovers for all types of furniture, as well as custom cushions for outdoor furniture, windowseat cushions and chair and bench cushions. I also do light upholstery such as dining chairs and benches.

Find Out More

Hi, I’m Terri Staten.

I’ve had a sewing business since 2007 making everything associated with home décor. In 2021, I decided to focus on slipcovers, cushions and pillows. I enjoy working with existing furniture to give it a new look and new life. I also love working with customers to find the perfect fabric to style a room, whether it’s one window seat cushion or an entire room redo. I look forward to working with you.
